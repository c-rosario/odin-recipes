Beef Patty

Delicous beef patties that will leave your family clamoring for more

This Jamaican beef patty recipe makes delicious curry-flavored beef pastries popular in Jamaica and other Caribbean islands. Try serving them with peas and rice, or have them as a snack.

Ingredients

Pastry:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1/3 cup water

Filling:

  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
